Shipping

  • General Shipping Policy

    The Dr. Martens U.S. website drmartens offers various shipping methods. We will do our best to process orders as soon as possible, however you may experience seasonal delays and we strongly encourage you to place your orders as early as possible. In some cases, orders may require more than one(1) business day to process.

    EXPEDITED SHIPPING:

    While these orders are prioritized, please anticipate at least one (1) business day for processing time.

    STANDARD SHIPPING:

    Please allow up to five (5) business days for processing times during holiday & sale periods.

    SHIPMENT CONFIRMATION:

    Once your order leaves the warehouse, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information and estimated arrival date.

    Your carrier will be listed on your shipment confirmation email. Orders are delivered Monday-Friday. Your carrier will be automatically selected based on the shipping address and cannot be changed. APO/FPO and Puerto Rico addresses are shipped via UPS SurePost and the US Postal Service. These shipments may take approximately 3 to 8 weeks to arrive. Check your confirmation email for tracking and timelines details. All "Delivery by" dates are estimates and not guaranteed. Shipping services and rates are subject to change without prior notice. UPS expedited options may not be available in all areas. Once your order leaves the warehouse, your carrier is responsible for the delivery of your package.  If your order is marked as delivered and you did not receive it, please check with neighbors and surrounding areas. Please contact us if you need assistance.

  • International Customers Do you ship to my country?

    We can ship to most countries! To find out if we can ship to your country, go to our international website here and click on the icon of a flag at the bottom of the page, it will give you the option to choose your country. Shipping costs are based on the item(s) in the order, the carrier selected, as well as any applicable duties and taxes for your country, so we're not able to provide a figure for that. However, you'll be able to fully review shipping costs before placing your order.

    Freight Forwarders

    As a precaution against fraud, we are unable to ship packages to freight forwarding addresses. We reserve the right to cancel any order shipping to a known freight forwarding address. If you are outside the United States, please consider using our international site to place your order.
