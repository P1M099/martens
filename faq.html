FAQs

  • Does Dr. Martens charge sales tax?

    Yes, Dr. Martens does collect sales tax in states that require online retailers to collect sales tax on orders shipped into that state.
    The tax rate applied will be based on the type of item purchased and will be the combined state and local rate for the address to which your order is shipped.

  • Why didn’t my payment go through?

    There are a few possible reasons why your payment didn't go through. Most often, the billing address provided does not match the "Master Address" that your issuing bank has on file.
    If your billing address is correct and the transaction still isn't going through, please contact us. Our customer service team will be able to diagnose the problem and provide a solution.
    In the event of a declined transaction, you may see pending charges on your account. These are authorization holds placed by your bank when the transaction is attempted; money has not been removed from your account. Authorization holds typically take about 1-2 business days to clear, however this timeline is dependent on your issuing bank. This process can often be expedited by contacting your bank.

  • Can I pay for my order with multiple credit cards?

    At this time, our online checkout system does not support payments using multiple credit cards. However, you can pay for your purchase using one credit card and one Dr. Martens gift card.

  • How do I redeem a Gift Card online?

    To redeem a physical or eGift card on drmartens.com, choose your payment method as “Dr. Martens eGift Card” on the payment page at checkout. Type your gift card number and security code into each field.
    Upon redemption, any unused balance will remain on the gift card for future purchases.

  • Where can I use my online gift card?

    eGift cards may only be purchased on the www.drmartens.com site. Physical gift cards may only be purchased in Dr. Martens retail stores in the United States.
    Both eGift and physical cards are redeemable both online and in Dr. Martens retail stores in the United States.

  • What do I do if the recipient didn't get the gift card?

    We recommend having the recipient check their spam or junk folder in case the gift card email was routed there by mistake.
    If they do not see it, please contact us so we may assist you.

  • Can I use multiple gift cards on the same order?

    Yes, you can use multiple Dr. Martens gift cards e-gift cards on the same order! Simply add the gift cards on the payment options screen to redeem them on your purchase!

  • What do I do if I received the wrong item?

    If you received an item that doesn't match what you ordered, please contact us with your order number and photos of the item that you received from us, so we may assist you.

    Once an order is placed, we are unable to cancel or change your order. Orders are immediately sent to our warehouse for processing.

  • What do I do if I received the wrong size?

    If you received a size different than from what you ordered, please contact us with photos of the item that you received from us including a picture of the inside of the tongue where the size is labeled, so we may assist you further.

    Please make sure the photos submitted are attached as .JPEGs.

    Please keep in mind that the easiest way to confirm the size that you received matched the size of the item that was ordered is to refer to the UK size on the packaging. Not all of our styles are going to show the size options for both US Men and US Women's on the packaging.

    1. - Our Made In England line will show the UK size on the tongue of the item, while the box will show the UK size and the US Men's size.
    2. - Our unisex styles are made with the UK, US Men's, and US Women's sizes printed on the box and tongue.
    3. - Our insoles typically will show the UK Size as well as the US Men's size shown as "US".
    4. - For all styles, the US Men's size is one higher than the UK size and the US Women’s size is two higher than the UK size. This also means that the US Women’s size is one higher than the US Men’s size.

    If you have not received an order number, please contact us, as your order may not have gone through successfully.

  • Can I see my order online if I used Guest Checkout?

    No. However, you will receive an order confirmation via email, as well as tracking information once the order ships. If you aren’t receiving these emails, it may be that you have entered an invalid email address or that they may have ended up in your spam / junk mail folder.
    If you are unable to locate your order confirmation email, please contact us so we may assist you.

    You may return your unworn purchase made on drmartens.com up to 30 days from your original order date for a refund.
    All returns must be processed through our returns page and must be in their original, unworn condition, with the shoebox intact and tags attached. We recommend returning your product in the container in which it was shipped to you.

    We do not offer exchanges. If you would like a different size or style, you may return your order and place a new order.

    Expedited shipping costs will not be refunded upon return. If you didn't receive your package within the timeframe provided at checkout, please contact us so we may assist you.

    Once we receive and process your return, you will be refunded to your original form of payment.

    If your package was marked as delivered but you have not received it, please contact us immediately. We will contact your carrier on your behalf and initiate a missing package investigation. This investigation may take up to 10 business days to conclude. Once the investigation concludes, we will either send you an expedited replacement package at no cost or process a full refund. Please reach out to us within 60 days of the delivery date if you have not received your order.
