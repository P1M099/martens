If you believe your Product has a defect covered by the Limited Warranty, to submit a valid warranty claim, you must contact us within the applicable Warranty Period and follow these steps: you must include (a) photographs of your Product showing the alleged defect, (b) a description of the alleged defect, and (c) a receipt or other proof of your purchase from Dr. Martens or one of its authorized resellers that identifies the seller and the transaction date.
