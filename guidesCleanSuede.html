How To Clean Suede Dr. Martens Footwear

  • The Boot Doctor Says: Our Suede Cleaner Will Revive Even The Most Well-Loved Pair Of Suede Shoes Or Boots

    Suede Upkeep Is Notoriously Tricky. But Looked After Properly, Suede Footwear Can Be A Long-Lasting Feature In Your Collection. Our Suede Cleaner Is Designed To Restore And Clean Your Suede Shoes To Their Former Glory, Whatever You’Ve Put Them Through. Here’S How To Use It — In Five Easy Steps — With A Few Tips From The Dr. Martens Boot Doctor. Check Out Our Full Video At The Bottom Of The Page.

  • Step 1 : Your Shoe Care Kit

    First up, find a spot you don’t mind getting a bit wet or flicked with mud. You’ll need a damp cloth or sponge, some clean water, a suede brush and your bottle of Dr. Martens Suede Cleaner. Find a playlist to keep you entertained for 20-30 minutes.

    Boot Doctor Tip: Taking Some Time To Look After Your Suede Makes All The Difference

  • Step 2 : Remove Dirt

    If you’ve been putting your boots through their paces, they might need a clean-up first. Whip out your laces to clean separately and wipe down the boots with a damp cloth.

    Boot Doctor Tip: If Your Boots Have Lived A Little, Bang Them Together To Remove Any Excess Mud First

  • Step 3 : Get Stuck In

    Scoop some polish out with a dry cloth, and apply a thin layer all over the boot, including the tongue. Pay attention to any scuffs, scratches or marks, and ensure you get a good, even coating. Wipe off any excess with a cloth.

  • Step 4 : Grab Your Suede Brush

    Once your boots are dry, use the soft rubber side of your brush to sweep lightly over the suede. Make sure you brush in the same direction to keep your suede looking as fresh as possible.

    Boot Doctor Tip: A New Pair Of Laces Can Add A Whole New Look

  • Step 5 : Re-Lace

    Pop your clean laces back in, or play around with a new pair. Suede makes a great canvas for some funky patterns and bright colours. Finish with a few sprays of Dr. Martens Ultra Protector, in case it’s a while before you sit down for your next spring clean.

    Boot Doctor Tip: Use Suede Cleaner Monthly To Prolong The Life Of Your Footwear
